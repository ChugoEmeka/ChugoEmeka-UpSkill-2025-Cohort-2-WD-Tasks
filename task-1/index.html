My Favourite Animal

My favourite animal is the dog, and here's why I like dogs

Dogs are known for their loyalty and companionship. They come in various breeds, each with its own unique characteristics and traits.
Dogs are often referred to as man's best friend because of their unwavering loyalty and ability to form strong bonds with humans. They are also highly trainable and can be taught a variety of commands and tricks.
Dogs require regular exercise and mental stimulation to stay happy and healthy. Overall, dogs make wonderful pets and bring joy to the lives of their owners.
